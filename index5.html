THE THREE-MINUTE SPEECH (3MT)
To see excellent examples of good public speeches, go to YouTube and watch winners of 3MT (Three Minute Thesis) competitions. The Three Minute Thesis competition is held in over 200 universities worldwide. It is open to PhD students, who have to present their research in just 180 seconds, so that it can be easily understood by an  audience with no background in the research area. It develops presentation, research and academic communication skills and develops research students' capacity to explain their work effectively.
